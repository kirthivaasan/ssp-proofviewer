Welcome to the CryptoZoo!

A place where state separating proofs (SSPs) live.

For a formal and rigorous introduction to SSPs please check out: BDFKK paper.

There are currently three case studies available:

  • IND-CPA
  • GGM PRF-from-PRG: We give a proof for the famous GGM Tree construction for constant depth. The proof uses a PRG and builds a PRF.
  • Garbling Security of Yao's Protocol: Brzuska and Oechsner formalized security of Yao's Garblind Scheme in the framework of SSPs. We show their proof in our tool.
