Welcome to the CryptoZoo!

A place where state separating proofs (SSPs) live.

For a formal and rigorous introduction to SSPs please check out: BDFKK paper.

There are currently three case studies available:

  • IND-CPA: Equivalence of Semantic Security. The proof uses the following definitions:
    • Indistinguishability chosen plaintext attack (IND-CPA)
    • Simulation-based IND-CPA
  • GGM PRF-from-PRG: We give a proof for the famous GGM Tree construction for constant depth. The proof uses the following definitions
    • (Length Doubling) Pseudo-Random Generators (PRG)
    • Pseudo-Random Functions (PRF)
  • Garbling Security of Yao's Protocol: Brzuska and Oechsner formalized security of Yao's Garblind Scheme in the framework of SSPs. We show their proof in our tool. The proof uses the following definitions
    • IND CPA?
